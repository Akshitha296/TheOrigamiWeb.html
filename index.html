Interesting facts about the swan!

  • They are huge! Trumpeter swans are the largest native waterfowl and the heaviest flying birds in North America.
  • They have their own constellation! How cool is that?!
  • Swans live up to their reputation of being very beautiful birds, so pretty they are!

Interesting facts about the butterfly!

  • Butterflies can't fly if they're cold. Butterflies actually need to maintain a surprisingly high body temperature if they want to fly around.
  • Butterfly wings are actually transparent! Thats cool! You can see through them!
  • Butterflies don't actually eat anything. How do they survive?!
